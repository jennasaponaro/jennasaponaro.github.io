Hi there, I'm Jenna.

I'm in my element when I'm meeting new people and helping bring a vision to life. At home I can be found whipping up new and random dishes in my kitchen and laughing as much as possible. Having served in the public sector for the past three years, coupled with recent work for a start-up marketing firm I am looking for a new challenge and adventure!

I live in Columbus, Ohio, but I'm originally from northern California. It’s been ten years since the transition but I'm still adjusting to the gray skies and never-ending winters. Nonetheless, Columbus is constantly changing, and it is my mission to explore all it has to offer.

Running a public office and managing clients has propelled me to a level of social that reaches Olympic status, and I wouldn’t change a thing. Getting to know people is how I work and make things happen. I hope this space helps to give you a glimpse into who I am. I'd love to discuss exciting, new opportunities, so feel free to email or message me.

Thanks for stopping by!
